How to Play

  • Setup: Start with 20 coins. Your King is auto-selected — place it first. Then buy & place more pieces on your starting rows. Limit: 2 of each piece, 1 Queen.
  • Start Battle: Both players must place their King. Click when ready — coins reset to 0.
  • Battle turn (buy first!): Optionally buy & place 1 card, then move 1 piece. Click "Skip → Move" to skip buying. Click "End Turn" to skip moving.
  • Win: Capture the opponent's King!
  • Costs & capture rewards — Pawn: 1  ·  Rook: 3  ·  Knight: 3  ·  Bishop: 3  ·  Queen: 5  ·  King: free
